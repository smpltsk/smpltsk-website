Data Deletion

Complete Control Over Your Data

Important: SMPLTSK stores ALL data locally on your device.

We do not collect, store, or have access to any of your photos, tasks, or personal information on our servers. Everything stays on your device, which means you have complete control over your data.

How to Delete Your Data

Since all SMPLTSK data is stored locally on your device, deleting your data is simple and immediate. Choose one of the methods below:

Method 1: Delete Individual Tasks (Partial Deletion)

To delete specific tasks while keeping the app:

  1. Open SMPLTSK on your device
  2. Navigate to the task you want to delete
  3. Tap the delete/trash icon or swipe to delete
  4. Confirm deletion

Result: Selected tasks and their associated photos are permanently deleted from your device.

Method 2: Clear All Data Within the App

To delete all your data while keeping the app installed:

  1. Open SMPLTSK
  2. Go to Settings (gear icon)
  3. Tap "Clear All Data" or "Delete All Tasks"
  4. Confirm that you want to delete all data

Result: All tasks, photos, and app data are permanently deleted. The app remains installed and ready to use with a fresh start.

Method 3: Uninstall the App (Complete Deletion)

For iOS:

  1. Press and hold the SMPLTSK app icon
  2. Tap "Remove App"
  3. Select "Delete App"
  4. Confirm deletion

For Android:

  1. Go to Settings → Apps
  2. Find and select SMPLTSK
  3. Tap "Uninstall"
  4. Confirm uninstallation

Result: The app and ALL associated data are permanently removed from your device.

⚠️ Important: Data Deletion is Permanent and Immediate

Once you delete data from SMPLTSK:

  • The deletion is immediate and permanent
  • There is no way to recover deleted data
  • We cannot help you restore data because we never had access to it
  • Make sure you've completed important tasks before deleting

What Gets Deleted?

When you delete data from SMPLTSK, the following is permanently removed:

  • All task photos you've captured
  • All markers and annotations on photos
  • All notes and comments you've added
  • Task organization and categories
  • App settings and preferences

What About Backups?

If you have device backups enabled (iCloud for iOS or Google Drive for Android):

  • SMPLTSK data may be included in your device backups
  • To ensure complete deletion, you may need to delete old device backups
  • Consult your device's backup settings for more information

We Never Had Your Data

Privacy by Design

SMPLTSK was built with privacy as a core principle. Because all data stays on your device:

  • We never collected your data in the first place
  • We cannot access your photos or tasks
  • There are no servers storing your information
  • You don't need to request data deletion from us
  • You have complete, immediate control
