Why SMPLTSK Exists

As a property owner and manager, I was constantly frustrated with traditional task apps. They all forced me to stop what I was doing, pull out my phone, and try to describe in text what I was looking at.

"Third gutter from the left needs cleaning." By the time I got back with the ladder, which gutter was it again?

"Outlet in bedroom not working." Which outlet? Which bedroom? The text never captured what I actually saw.

The problem was obvious: our brains think visually, but task apps force us to think in text. That translation step is where tasks get forgotten, details get lost, and time gets wasted.

SMPLTSK changes everything. See a broken fence post? Snap it. Mark it. Done. See a gutter full of leaves? Snap it. Mark it. Done. No typing. No describing. No forgetting.

For homeowners, property managers, contractors, maintenance teams, and anyone who thinks visually - this is the task manager that finally matches how your brain actually works.

Save time. Save money. Get things done.

How It Works

Three simple steps to never forget a task again

1

Capture

See something that needs attention? Open SMPLTSK and take a photo. That's it. The task is captured exactly as it exists in the real world.

2

Mark

Tap the photo to place a marker exactly where the task is. Add a quick note if needed. The visual marker makes it impossible to forget what needs doing.

3

Complete

When you're ready to tackle the task, open SMPLTSK, see exactly what needs doing, and mark it complete. Your visual reference is always there.
