The Problem

Why SMPLTSK Exists

The Australian construction industry is facing a productivity challenge: many tradespeople can see a problem and solve it, but struggle with text-heavy systems that don't match how they naturally think and work.

The solution is in front of our eyes: We all know a picture is worth a thousand words. COMMUNICATE WITH PICTURES.

The Founder's Story: Living the Problem Daily

"As a registered builder, I need to ensure that client requests are clearly understood by my tradespeople. When I don't communicate effectively errors occur and costs and frustration rise. SMPLTSK was designed to reduce the mental load on my workers. I wanted to work smarter, make things simpler and save valuable time."

SMPLTSK bridges the gap between how people see problems and how they communicate solutions. Every forgotten task is wasted time. Every miscommunication is a wasted trip. Every repeated site visit is money down the drain. Visual task management matches the way most brains actually work.

Simple Process

How It Works

Place numbered markers on photos, add descriptions, send to your team

You can send a task in under a minute.

1

See It

Take a photo of the task. Capture what needs to be done exactly where it needs to happen.

Upload: step1-see-it.png

2

Mark It

Tap the screen to place numbered markers on your photo. Add task descriptions to each marker and send to your team.

Upload: step2-mark-it.png

3

Do It

Receiver taps each marker to read the task description, completes the work, and marks it done (turns green). No confusion, no forgotten tasks.

Upload: step3-do-it.png
