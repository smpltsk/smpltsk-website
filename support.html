Frequently Asked Questions

How do I get started with SMPLTSK?

Getting started is simple:

  1. Download the app from the App Store or Google Play
  2. Open the app (no account needed!)
  3. Take a photo of something that needs attention
  4. Tap to mark the specific area
  5. Add a quick note if needed
  6. Mark as complete when done

Is my data private and secure?

Yes, completely! All your photos and tasks stay on your device. We never collect, access, or transmit your data. There's no cloud storage, no servers, and no accounts. What happens on your phone stays on your phone.

How do I delete my data?

You have full control over your data. Visit our Data Deletion Guide for complete instructions on deleting individual tasks, clearing all data, or removing the app entirely.

Do I need an internet connection?

No! SMPLTSK works completely offline. Since everything is stored locally on your device, you can capture, organize, and complete tasks anywhere, anytime.

How do I back up my data?

Since data is stored locally, use your device's backup system:

  • iOS: Enable iCloud backup in Settings
  • Android: Enable Google Drive backup in Settings

This ensures your SMPLTSK data is included in your regular device backups.

What happens if I uninstall the app?

Uninstalling SMPLTSK will delete all your photos and tasks from the app. Make sure you've completed important tasks or have a device backup before uninstalling.

Can I use SMPLTSK on multiple devices?

You can install SMPLTSK on multiple devices, but data doesn't sync between them (since we don't have cloud storage). Each device maintains its own local data.

Can I share tasks with others?

Currently, SMPLTSK is designed for personal use with local storage. You can share individual photos using your device's standard sharing options, but task data won't transfer.

What devices are supported?

SMPLTSK is available for:

  • iOS 14.0 and later
  • Android 8.0 and later

Is SMPLTSK free?

Check the App Store or Google Play for current pricing. We believe in transparent, straightforward pricing with no hidden fees or subscriptions.

How do I delete my data?

You can delete data in two ways:

  • Delete individual tasks within the app
  • Uninstall the app to remove all data

Remember: we never store your data on our servers, so uninstalling completely removes all SMPLTSK data from existence.

Common Troubleshooting Steps

  1. App won't open: Restart your device and try again. Ensure you have the latest version of the app.
  2. Camera not working: Check that SMPLTSK has camera permissions in your device settings (Settings ‚Üí Privacy ‚Üí Camera).
  3. Photos not saving: Ensure your device has sufficient storage space available.
  4. App crashes: Update to the latest version from the App Store/Play Store. If the problem persists, email us at support@smpltsk.com
  5. Lost data after update: Check if you have a device backup you can restore from. Contact support if you need assistance.
